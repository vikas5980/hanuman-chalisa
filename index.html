Hanuman Chalisa

हनुमान चालीसा

BY GOSWAMI TULSIDAS

दोहा — Opening Couplet
श्रीगुरु चरन सरोज रज, निज मनु मुकुरु सुधारि।
बरनउँ रघुबर बिमल जसु, जो दायकु फल चारि॥
With the dust of Sri Guru's lotus feet, I cleanse the mirror of my mind,
and narrate the pure glory of Sri Rama — the giver of four fruits of life.
दोहा — Opening Couplet 2
बुद्धिहीन तनु जानिके, सुमिरौं पवन-कुमार।
बल बुधि बिद्या देहु मोहि, हरहु कलेस बिकार॥
Knowing myself to be without intelligence, I remember Pavan Kumar (Hanuman).
Grant me strength, wisdom and knowledge, and remove my afflictions and impurities.
चौपाई — Verses 1–4
1जय हनुमान ज्ञान गुन सागर। जय कपीस तिहुँ लोक उजागर॥
Victory to Hanuman, ocean of wisdom and virtue. Victory to the Lord of monkeys, illuminator of the three worlds.
2राम दूत अतुलित बल धामा। अंजनि पुत्र पवनसुत नामा॥
Messenger of Rama, abode of incomparable strength. Son of Anjani, known as the son of the wind.
3महाबीर बिक्रम बजरंगी। कुमति निवार सुमति के संगी॥
Great hero, valiant, with a body like lightning. Remover of evil thoughts, companion of good intellect.
4कंचन बरन बिराज सुबेसा। कानन कुंडल कुँचित केसा॥
Golden-hued, adorned in splendid attire. Wearing ear-rings and with curly hair.
चौपाई — Verses 5–8
5हाथ बज्र औ ध्वजा बिराजै। काँधे मूँज जनेऊ साजै॥
In hands the thunderbolt and flag shine. The sacred thread of munja grass adorns the shoulder.
6संकर सुवन केसरीनंदन। तेज प्रताप महा जग बंदन॥
Incarnation of Shankara, son of Kesari. Of great glory and splendor, worshipped by the whole world.
7बिद्यावान गुनी अति चातुर। राम काज करिबे को आतुर॥
Learned, virtuous and very clever. Eager to do the work of Rama.
8प्रभु चरित्र सुनिबे को रसिया। राम लखन सीता मन बसिया॥
Delighted in listening to the Lord's deeds. Rama, Lakshmana, and Sita reside in your heart.
चौपाई — Verses 9–12
9सूक्ष्म रूप धरि सियहिं दिखावा। बिकट रूप धरि लंक जरावा॥
Taking a tiny form, you appeared before Sita. Taking a fearsome form, you burned Lanka.
10भीम रूप धरि असुर सँहारे। रामचंद्र के काज सँवारे॥
Taking an awesome form, you slew demons. You accomplished the tasks of Ramachandra.
11लाय सजीवन लखन जियाये। श्रीरघुबीर हरषि उर लाये॥
Bringing the Sanjeevani herb, you revived Lakshmana. Sri Raghuvira joyfully embraced you.
12रघुपति कीन्ही बहुत बड़ाई। तुम मम प्रिय भरतहि सम भाई॥
Raghupati praised you greatly: "You are as dear to me as my brother Bharata."
चौपाई — Verses 13–16
13सहस बदन तुम्हरो जस गावैं। अस कहि श्रीपति कंठ लगावैं॥
Thousands of mouths sing your glory — saying this, the Lord of Lakshmi embraced you.
14सनकादिक ब्रह्मादि मुनीसा। नारद सारद सहित अहीसा॥
Sages like Sanaka, Brahma and other great sages — Narada, Sarada, and Shesha (Ahisa) too —
15जम कुबेर दिगपाल जहाँ ते। कबि कोबिद कहि सके कहाँ ते॥
Yama, Kubera, the guardians of directions — poets and learned men cannot fully describe your glory.
16तुम उपकार सुग्रीवहिं कीन्हा। राम मिलाय राज पद दीन्हा॥
You did a great favor to Sugriva. Having him meet Rama, you gave him his kingdom.
चौपाई — Verses 17–20
17तुम्हरो मंत्र बिभीषन माना। लंकेस्वर भए सब जग जाना॥
Vibhishana heeded your counsel, and became Lord of Lanka — the whole world knows this.
18जुग सहस्र जोजन पर भानू। लील्यो ताहि मधुर फल जानू॥
The sun, thousands of yojanas away, you swallowed thinking it a sweet fruit.
19प्रभु मुद्रिका मेलि मुख माहीं। जलधि लाँघि गये अचरज नाहीं॥
Keeping the Lord's ring in your mouth, you leaped the ocean — no wonder!
20दुर्गम काज जगत के जेते। सुगम अनुग्रह तुम्हरे तेते॥
All the difficult tasks of the world become easy by your grace.
चौपाई — Verses 21–24
21राम दुआरे तुम रखवारे। होत न आज्ञा बिनु पैसारे॥
You are the guardian of Rama's door. None can enter without your permission.
22सब सुख लहै तुम्हारी सरना। तुम रक्षक काहू को डर ना॥
All comforts are obtained in your shelter. With you as protector, there is nothing to fear.
23आपन तेज सम्हारो आपै। तीनों लोक हाँक तें काँपै॥
You alone can control your own brilliance. The three worlds tremble at your roar.
24भूत पिसाच निकट नहिं आवै। महाबीर जब नाम सुनावै॥
Ghosts and evil spirits do not come near when the name of Mahavira is uttered.
चौपाई — Verses 25–28
25नासै रोग हरै सब पीरा। जपत निरंतर हनुमत बीरा॥
Diseases perish and all pains are removed — by constantly chanting the name of brave Hanuman.
26संकट तें हनुमान छुड़ावै। मन क्रम बचन ध्यान जो लावै॥
Hanuman frees from all troubles one who meditates on him in thought, deed, and word.
27सब पर राम तपस्वी राजा। तिन के काज सकल तुम साजा॥
Rama reigns supreme as an ascetic king. You accomplish all his tasks.
28और मनोरथ जो कोई लावै। सोइ अमित जीवन फल पावै॥
Whoever brings any desire before you receives unlimited fruits of life.
चौपाई — Verses 29–32
29चारों जुग परताप तुम्हारा। है परसिद्ध जगत उजियारा॥
Your glory is spread across all four ages. Your radiance illuminates the world.
30साधु-संत के तुम रखवारे। असुर निकंदन राम दुलारे॥
You are the guardian of saints and sages. Destroyer of demons, and the beloved of Rama.
31अष्टसिद्धि नौ निधि के दाता। अस बर दीन जानकी माता॥
You are the giver of the eight siddhis and nine treasures — this boon was granted by Mother Janaki.
32राम रसायन तुम्हरे पासा। सदा रहो रघुपति के दासा॥
You hold the elixir of Ram's name. You remain ever the servant of Raghupati.
चौपाई — Verses 33–36
33तुम्हरे भजन राम को पावै। जनम जनम के दुख बिसरावै॥
By your devotion one attains Rama and forgets sorrows of countless births.
34अंत काल रघुबर पुर जाई। जहाँ जन्म हरिभक्त कहाई॥
At the time of death, one goes to Raghuvira's abode and is born ever as a devotee of Hari.
35और देवता चित्त न धरई। हनुमत सेइ सर्ब सुख करई॥
You need not contemplate any other deity. Serving Hanuman bestows all happiness.
36संकट कटै मिटै सब पीरा। जो सुमिरै हनुमत बलबीरा॥
All troubles cease and all pain vanishes for one who remembers Hanuman, the powerful hero.
चौपाई — Verses 37–40
37जय जय जय हनुमान गोसाईं। कृपा करहु गुरुदेव की नाईं॥
Victory, victory, victory to Lord Hanuman. Bestow your grace as a spiritual master.
38जो सत बार पाठ कर कोई। छूटहि बंदि महा सुख होई॥
Whoever recites this a hundred times is released from bondage and attains great happiness.
39जो यह पढ़ै हनुमान चालीसा। होय सिद्धि साखी गौरीसा॥
Whoever reads this Hanuman Chalisa — Lord Shiva himself is witness — attains perfection.
40तुलसीदास सदा हरि चेरा। कीजै नाथ हृदय मह डेरा॥
Tulsidas, always a servant of Hari, prays: O Lord, make my heart your abode.
दोहा — Closing Couplet
पवन तनय संकट हरन, मंगल मूरति रूप।
राम लखन सीता सहित, हृदय बसहु सुर भूप॥
O Son of the Wind, remover of afflictions, embodiment of auspiciousness —
dwell in my heart together with Rama, Lakshmana, and Sita, O King of Gods.
